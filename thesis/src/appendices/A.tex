\chapter{3D-Printed Component Models}\label{app:3d_models}

To ensure the proposed open-source robotic platform is fully and easily reproducible, all custom structural and mechanical components were designed parametrically for additive manufacturing. Rather than providing static 2D technical drawings, the complete geometry is provided in the form of stereolithography (\texttt{.STL}) files. 

These files are included in the attached archive to this thesis and are also archived in the public project repository at \url{https://github.com/knedl1k/decaf}. 

\Cref{tab:stl_files} lists the provided digital models, categorized by their respective hardware subsystems as detailed in \Cref{chap:hw}.
